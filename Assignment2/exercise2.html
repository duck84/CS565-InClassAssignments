placekeanu

A quick and simple service for getting pictures of kittens
for use as placeholders in your designs or code. Just put
your image size (width & height) after our URL and you'll get
a placeholder.

Like this: http://placekeanu.com/200/300
or: http://placekeanu.com/g/200/300
